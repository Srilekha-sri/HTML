why we started this

  • Save a life: Adopting a dog gives an animal a second chance at life in a loving home.
  • Reduce pet overpopulation: Adopting a dog helps break the cycle of pet overpopulation.
  • Cost-effective: Adopting a dog is often cheaper than buying a dog from a breeder.
  • Access to helpful shelter staff: Shelter staff can help with questions about how to care for, train, and love a dog.
  • Get healthier: A dog can be an exercise buddy and help motivate you to stay fit.
  • Discover a community: Adopting a rescue dog can help you build a community with other dog owners.
  • Dogs give protection: Dogs can make you feel more at home and safe.
  • Enrich your life: Dogs can be wonderful companions, but they do require time and attention.
